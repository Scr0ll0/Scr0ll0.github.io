About Me

Darren Tung is currently a sophomore at Stony Brook University double majoring in Applied Mathematics and Statistics and Computer Science. Currently serving as a treasurer for SBU's branch of the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA-SBU), Darren knows what proper record-keeping and account management entails: teamwork, proactivity. scheduling, and communication. He also experimented with daily fantasy sports throughout 2020, creating Python lineup optimizers and applying investment strategies to turn $5 into $300 over the course of six months. In addition, Darren has partaken in research in fat/water analysis at SBU's radiology center, recieving training in MATLAB, VBA, and poster creation in the process. Darren hopes to pursue data science either with a career or with research. Besides his experience with computing, Darren is also a musician, versed in piano and clarinet.
